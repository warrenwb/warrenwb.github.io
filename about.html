Alex is a versatile and experienced presenter based in West Yorkshire who has appeared on Sky, Ch4, TVE2, BBC and ITV presenting a variety of TV genres from property to travel and lifestyle.

Experienced in location shoots and live studio based programmes, she has a wealth of expertise as live unscripted and pre-recorded presenting, writing her own scripts, using auto cue and talkback as well as green screen and pieces to camera.
As a well rounded presenter she has done "almost" everything from interviewing high profile celebrities in red carpet situations to presenting in winter contitions whilst gorge scrambling!


BACKGROUND
Alex grew up in rural North Yorkshire, a direct descendant of Leeds based engineering entrepreneurs George Bray (Bray’s Burners) and John McLaren (McLaren traction engines). Beginning her career as a senior graphic designer she soon turned her sights to television and on completing a first-class film, theatre and TV degree, relocated to London to work with some of the biggest production companies in the country.
Working as Line Producer/Production Exec she has over a decade of experience spanning across entertainment, docu-drama and specialist factual formats for all major UK and worldwide broadcasters. Credits include; ‘The Apprentice’ (BBC1), ‘David Attenborough’s The Link’ (BBC1/History), ‘The Wildest Dream’ (IMAX/Nat Geo), ‘Excluded at Seven’ (Ch4), Corona Virus: How to Isolate Yourself (Ch4) and ‘Our Baby: A Modern Miracle’ (Ch4). Her experience working in production has been invaluable for directors working with her as a presenter.

Working as a presenter and commercial model, Alex has appeared on various TV and branded formats, but it was her background in fashion that led her to present hours of live shows as stylist on QVC, travelling abroad and working for fashion brand La Redute whilst also reporting at events such as London Fashion Week. Creating her own style videos and blogging about the latest trends she both hosted and produced her own online menswear TV show MNSWR.TV.
