Alex is an experienced, multi skilled TV presenter based in central London who has presented a wealth of genres spanning property, travel, factual and entertainment. Interviewing celebrities and working in live, as-live and studio locations she has appeared in programmes including That’s Britain (TVE2), Ch4 Mash Up, Jamie’s Dream School (Ch4), Property Showcase (Sky 192) and Brit Asia (Sky 824).

Being a fashion blogger, reporter and stylist with experience as a retail manager, Alex's passion is fashion. Submersed in the fashion world and regular attendee at Fashion Week she is an authority on the latest trends and designers. She is also a regular fashion guest presenter on the live TV show QVC (Sky 650/678), a fashion reporter for South West London TV and fashion brand La Redoute. She also hosts and produces her own online menswear TV show MNSWR.TV.

Working in the UK and abroad she has interviewed high profile celebrities and fashion players such as David Gandy, Tinie Tempah, Hilary Alexander, Jean-Pierre Braganza, Zandra Rhodes, Christopher Raeburn, Susie Bubble, Laura Whitmore, Ricky Wilson and Dynamo to name a few.

With a first class honours degree in Film & TV (with Journalism), Alex started her television career working her way up the ladder behind the camera on shows for the likes of BBC, Ch4 and Nat Geo. Experienced working in production she is well versed in the entire production process and having written her own scripts, done pieces to camera and used auto cue, green screen and talkback she is completely unfazed in any environment.

In addition to the above, Alex has also worked as a commercial model and appeared in adverts such as Sagres Beer and Flora and presented numerous corporate videos.

In her past life, Alex grew up on a farm in Yorkshire driving diggers, rock climbing and riding horses, and having completed the Duke of Edinburgh award became keen on fitness and healthy foods which she maintains today.

With programme ideas up her sleeve, Alex is now looking to do more factual or factual/entertainment based TV presenting.
